Design a complete, production-quality UI/UX for a modern SaaS application called NRT AI Finance Manager.

This is NOT a generic finance dashboard.

It is an AI Employee that acts as a virtual Finance Manager for SMEs.

The final UI must look like it was designed by a senior product designer at Linear, Stripe, Notion, Ramp, Mercury, Arc, Vercel, or Apple.

The interface must NEVER look AI-generated.

The design must feel handcrafted, premium, polished, balanced, elegant, and highly usable.

Every screen should look production-ready and suitable for immediate frontend development.
